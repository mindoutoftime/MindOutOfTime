Saturday October 25th 2025 - Midway Saloon - halloween party - 9pm to midnight
 “Play it fuckin’ loud!” commands Bob Dylan. The three music scene vets in Mind Out of Time take that to heart, kicking out Bob Dylan jams without the coffee shop aura. The power trio rips through Dylan's catalog with rough edges and rowdy charm. True to Bob's live show ethos, what you hear on the record might not be what you'll hear on stage.
